Story Synopsis

If you had the chance to start over, what kind of life would you choose?

The brief human lifespan meant I had to leave this world early, leaving behind two beloved wives and the family I treasured most.

In that moment, I yearned desperatelyâ€”if only I could share more time with them again, protect them once more...

Miraculously, I was reborn into this world with memories of my previous life.

A new life, new childhood friends, and the wives and daughters who still exist.

However, the gears of fate have quietly begun to turn, and a new chapter is about to unfold.

This time, can I change the future and protect those I deeply love? Or is fate already predetermined, inescapable?

A romantic comedy that traverses time and space, weaving together past and present lives, different perspectives, and otherworldly adventures.

This is not just a story about love and memories, but a journey of defying destiny itself.

Reading Guide

This is a work filled with romantic and comedic elements. The story travels between different timelines, memories, and past lives, involving countries, other worlds, and multiple perspective changes. Readers must immerse themselves in each character's inner world rather than understanding the story solely from their own perspective. Please pay special attention to the timeline and perspective changes in each chapter to feel this moving story that spans across time and space.
